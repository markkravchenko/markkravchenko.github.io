About the work

Patience,
light, and
a long lens.

I'm a photographer based in Ontario with a background in cinematography. My work focuses on the intersection of landscape and activity — the moment a line hits the water, the first smoke rising from a camp stove, the blue hour on a northern lake. I shoot primarily across Algonquin, Muskoka, and the Kawarthas.

Specialty

Freshwater Fishing

Bass, pike, walleye — intimate frames from riverbanks and canoe gunwales across northern Ontario.

Specialty

Wilderness Camping

Portage routes, backcountry sites, and the slow rituals of camp life documented in natural light.

Specialty

Cinematic Landscape

Wide-angle golden hours, aerial perspectives, and long-exposure water — Ontario's geography at its most cinematic.
